The colors that I like the most and that I found interesting is red color. Red is the color of extremes. Red color of passionate love, seduction, violence, danger, anger, and adventure. Our prehistoric ancestors saw red as the color of fire and blood – energy and primal life forces – and most of red's symbolism today arises from its powerful associations in the past. Red was almost as rare and as expensive as purple in ancient days – a fact that may explain its magic and power.
