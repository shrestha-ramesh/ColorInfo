"About Grey"

Grey is a cool, neutral, and balanced color. The color grey is an emotionless, moody color that is typically associated with meanings of dull, dirty, and dingy, as well as formal, conservative, and sophisticated.

The color grey is a timeless and practical color that is often associated with loss or depression. Dark, charcoal grey communicates some of the strength and mystery of black. It is a sophisticated color that lack the negativity of the color black. Light greys can carry some of the attributes of the color white.

The grey color affects the mind and body by causing unsettling feelings. Light greys are feminine in nature, while dark greys are masculine in nature.

Positive and Negative Traits of Grey

  1. Positive keywords include: reliable, conservative, dignified, neutral, impartial, professional, mature, intelligent, classic, solid, stable, calming, sudued, reserved, elegant, formal and dependable.
  2. Negative keywords include: indecisive, non-emotional, indifferent, boring, sad, depressed, lifeless, lonely, isolated.
